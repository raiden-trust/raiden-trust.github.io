ABOUT

The Raiden Trust manages brainbot labs Establishment's Raiden Network grant programme. It is funded with 16 million RDN tokens and its goal is to support contributions to the wider Raiden ecosystem.
It is not involved with the core roadmap of the Raiden Network protocol, but rather aims to support projects and individuals that wish to contribute to the wider Raiden ecosystem.
